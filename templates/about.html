About the System

Web-Based Automated Symptom Diagnosis and Patient Recommendation System

Project Aim

To develop a user-friendly web-based automated symptom diagnosis and patient recommendation system tailored to the Nigerian healthcare context, improving early detection of diseases, enhancing access to reliable health information, reducing delays in medical diagnosis, and supporting more efficient healthcare service delivery.

How It Works

Our Hybrid AI engine analyzes symptoms using Rule-Based + Naive Bayes

4

Get Recommendations

Receive personalized healthcare advice and referral guidance

Hybrid AI Technology

The system uses a hybrid artificial intelligence model that combines:

  • Rule-Based Engine: Uses predefined medical rules and symptom-disease mappings with confidence weights
  • Naive Bayes Classifier: Probabilistic machine learning algorithm that calculates disease probabilities based on symptom patterns
  • Weighted Fusion: Combines both approaches using a tunable alpha parameter (default 0.6) for optimal accuracy

Important Disclaimer

This system provides preliminary diagnosis only. It is not a substitute for professional medical advice, diagnosis, or treatment. Always seek the advice of your physician or other qualified health provider with any questions you may have regarding a medical condition. Never disregard professional medical advice or delay in seeking it because of something you have read on this system.
